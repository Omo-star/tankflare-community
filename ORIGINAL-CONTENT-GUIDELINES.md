# Original Content Guidelines

When [contributing](CONTRIBUTING.md), please make sure the information is based on **your own experience** whenever possible.

It is okay to include information you learned from another keeper, breeder, friend, or reliable care resource. However, do not blatantly copy-paste a care sheet you found online. Contributions should help make TankFlare better by adding real keeper experience, not duplicated information from other websites.

Copied or poorly checked information can hurt TankFlare’s data and may lead to inaccurate results instead of helping the project.

If you are referencing care sheets, please make sure **the majority of the knowledge is coming from your own experience**. A good goal is around **70–80%+ original knowledge**. You should also cross-reference multiple sources and choose reliable resources to help keep the data accurate.

In addition, we encourage you to **avoid using AI as your main source of information**. AI can sound confident, but it may pull from outdated, unreliable, or unknown sources. For example, it could repeat advice from an old forum post or an unverified care sheet without making that clear.

AI may be useful for grammar, formatting, or organizing your writing, but the actual care information should come from real keeper experience and reliable sources.
