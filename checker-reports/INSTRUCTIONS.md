# TankFlare Stocking Calc Report Template Guide

> **Please read the [general contributing guide](https://github.com/Omo-star/tankflare-community/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) first!**

If the stocking calculator gives an incorrect result for your tank plan, submit a report using this template. If your report is approved, the case will be fixed quickly.

Remember: the checker gets stronger with every report!

## Filename

The filename should be the result the checker gave you, followed by a hyphen, followed by the result you expected.

Examples:

```text
RECOMMENDED-RECOMMENDED.md
RISKY-RECOMMENDED.md
NOT RECOMMENDED-RISKY.md
```

## Annotated Template

Each heading in the template is a question you should answer. An example response is shown after each section.

### `Stocking link?`

Paste the link you get after clicking **Copy Tank Plan Link** under the **Save My Tank** section.

```text
## Stocking link?

https://www.tankflare.com/checker/share/V8nEuUT_
```

### `How is the stocking calc wrong, and why?`

Give a thorough description of what result you expected, what result the checker gave, and why you believe the checker is wrong.

Relevant URLs are welcome, especially sources that support your argument.

```text
## How is the stocking calc wrong, and why?

I expected NOT RECOMMENDED, but got RECOMMENDED. Bettas generally should not be housed together, especially males, due to aggression and territorial behavior.

Source:

- https://www.seriouslyfish.com/species/betta-splendens/
```
